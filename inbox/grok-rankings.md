# Comprehensive Investment Analysis: High-Quality Compounders in Tech, Financials, Travel, and Semiconductors (Revised Edition – November 17, 2025)

## Summarized Company Theses

**Alphabet (GOOGL) – Strong Long**
Widest moat on earth (Search defaults, YouTube scale, Android ecosystem) now supercharged by Gemini/agentic AI across products. Q3 2025 $100B+ quarter showcased Cloud +34% and AI Mode driving incremental queries. **Linchpin**: Cloud backlog conversion + AI ad uplift. **Thesis breakers**: DOJ forces Android/Search divestiture (25% probability material impact) or OpenAI erodes Cloud share. Fair value $380 (+33% from ~$285).

**Moody's (MCO) – Strong Long**
Pure-play ratings duopoly + AI-enhanced Analytics. Private credit issuance +50% YoY and agentic solutions underappreciated. **Linchpin**: Private credit penetration + Analytics ARR >12%. **Thesis breakers**: Severe recession collapses issuance >20% or regulatory erosion of NRSRO status. Fair value $610 (+26% from ~$485).

**NVIDIA (NVDA) – Strong Long**
~95% AI GPU share, $500B backlog, Blackwell efficiency gains. **Linchpin**: Blackwell margin recovery + sovereign AI demand. **Thesis breakers**: Custom ASICs erode share >20% or AI ROI disappointment triggers capex digestion. Fair value $280 (+50% from ~$186).

**Booking Holdings (BKNG) – Strong Long**
OTA leader with merchant shift and Connected Trip flywheel. **Linchpin**: Flights/attachments cross-sell + merchant mix >50%. **Thesis breakers**: 2026 recession hits discretionary travel >15% or Google zero-click erodes traffic. Fair value $6,800 (+35% from ~$5,050).

**Amazon (AMZN) – Strong Long**
AWS AI re-acceleration + e-commerce robotics/regionalization. **Linchpin**: Trainium/Bedrock inference economics + ads >20%. **Thesis breakers**: CapEx overspend without ROI or antitrust marketplace breakup. Fair value $320 (+35% from ~$238).

**TSMC (TSM) – Long**
Indispensable advanced-node foundry. **Linchpin**: AI/HPC >40% CAGR + 2nm yield success. **Thesis breakers**: Taiwan geopolitical escalation or competitors close node gap. Fair value $380 (+35% from ~$282).

**KLA (KLAC) – Strong Long (Equipment Winner)**
Process control intensity tailwind. **Linchpin**: Inspection/metrology >20% of WFE long-term. **Thesis breakers**: China controls tighten further or WFE digestion. Fair value ~$1,400 (+24% from ~$1,130).

**Meta (META) – Long**
AI engagement flywheel (Llama, Reels, Meta AI >1B). **Linchpin**: Assistant-driven ad uplift. **Thesis breakers**: EU consent models -20% revenue or Llama commoditization. Fair value $780 (+28% from ~$610).

**Applied Materials (AMAT) – Long**
Broadest equipment portfolio. **Linchpin**: Leading-edge DRAM/HBM ramp. **Thesis breakers**: China revenue cliff deepens. Fair value ~$280 (+30% from ~$215).

**Lam Research (LRCX) – Long**
Etch/deposition strength in GAA/HBM. **Linchpin**: NAND conversions + foundry share gains. **Thesis breakers**: Cleanroom constraints delay ramps. Fair value ~$190 (+27% from ~$150).

**S&P Global (SPGI) – Hold**
Diversified but conglomerate discount vs. MCO. **Linchpin**: Private markets + MI re-acceleration. **Thesis breakers**: Mobility spin confusion or issuance normalization. Fair value $595 (+22% from ~$490).

**ASML – Hold**
EUV/High-NA monopoly with memory/HBM cushion offsetting China cliff. **Linchpin**: High-NA shipments + China floor >20%. **Thesis breakers**: Major logic customers delay >12 months or export bans expand. Fair value ~$1,100 (+9% from ~$1,010).

**Airbnb (ABNB) – Hold**
Regulatory-constrained alt-accom vs. BKNG. **Linchpin**: “Beyond core” ramp. **Thesis breakers**: Urban supply bans accelerate. Fair value $145 (+15% from ~$126).

**IonQ – Avoid**
Technical fidelity lead but no economic moat, tiny revenue, massive burn. **Linchpin**: Fault-tolerant logical qubits by 2028-30. **Thesis breakers**: Physics bottlenecks persist (80%+ probability). Risk-adjusted fair value $12-15 (-70-75% from ~$48).
